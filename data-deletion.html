Account & Data Deletion

How to remove your data from Kauai Beaches

Good News: Kauai Beaches does not require user accounts. All data is processed locally on your device or anonymously, which means we don't store your personal information on our servers.

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Delete the App

Simply uninstall Kauai Beaches from your device. This removes all local data stored by the app, including:

  • Your preferences and settings
  • Any cached weather data
  • App usage history stored on your device

Option 2: Request Data Deletion

If you believe we have any personal data associated with your account, you can request deletion by emailing:

privacy@beachchairindustries.com

Please include:

  • Your device identifier (if available)
  • The email address you may have used to contact us
  • Any other information that would help us locate your data

What Happens After a Deletion Request

  • We will acknowledge your request within 7 business days
  • We will complete the deletion within 30 days
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete

Data We Cannot Delete

Some data may be retained for legal or operational reasons:

  • Anonymized analytics data that cannot be linked back to you
  • Records required by law (such as payment records for tax purposes)
  • Data in backup systems (which will be deleted when backups expire)

Questions?

If you have questions about data deletion, contact us at: privacy@beachchairindustries.com
